Blocked phones Open on Hyperskill

Sometimes unknown people call you and try to suggest their services. This can bother you.

To solve the problem you must implement the filterPhones method that returns only phones that are not in the black list.

[HINT]The simplest way to solve it is to use the contains method, for-each loop and one additional collection.[/HINT]

 

Sample Input:
387-968-2999 505-468-5235 739-738-3272
505-468-5235
Sample Output:
387-968-2999 739-738-3272
